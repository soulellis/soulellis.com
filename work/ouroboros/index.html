Ouroboros (2022)



I worked with Image Text Ithacan to edit, design, and produce Ouroboros, the thesis publication of the ITI MFA class of 2022. The six-month process was highly collaborative, and culminated in the 144-page magazine-style publication, organized into two sections: “there is no apparent system,” and “only a map of feeling.” We also curated and designed a corresponding exhibition at Essex Flowers in NYC in August 2022. Ouroboros also launched at Printed Matter’s NY Art Book Fair in October. Publication photographs by Hyacinth Schukis.
