“Urgent Publishing After the Artist’s Book: Making Public in Movements Towards Liberation,” was presented February 27 at 1:30PM for The 2021 Contemporary Artists’s Book Conference, hosted by Center for Book Arts, which took place during Printed Matter’s first-ever Virtual Art Book Fair. A written version, with slides, is available here. This talk will also be delivered at The Centre for Postdigital Cultures, Coventry University, England on June 11, 2021.



On Publishing: Graphic Designers Who Publish (January 2021) is a new project by Jon Sueda, Will Ruby, and Chris Hamamoto, featuring interviews with graphic designers who involve themselves with publishing as part of their practice. Featuring 24 imprints, the interviews inquire about the motivation and challenges of this endeavor and include a range of studios from around the world. Happy to be in such good company, along with Danielle Aubert, Colpa Press, Emigre, Gato Negro, and Kristian Henson, among others.
