Paul Soulellis is an artist and educator based in Providence, RI. His practice includes teaching, writing, and experimental publishing, with a focus on queer methodologies and network culture. He is founder/director of Queer.Archive.Work, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit community reading room, publishing studio, and project space, and Associate Professor of Graphic Design at Rhode Island School of Design.
