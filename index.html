Paul Soulellis is an artist and educator based in Providence, RI. His practice includes teaching, writing, and experimental publishing, with a focus on queer methodologies and network culture. He is founder/director of Queer.Archive.Work, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit reading room, publishing studio, and community space, and Associate Professor of Graphic Design at Rhode Island School of Design. Talks & interviews. News. CV. Studio—40 Sprague Street, 1G Providence, RI 02907. paul@soulellis.com @soulellis
