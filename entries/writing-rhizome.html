Rhizome (2015–2017)

In 2014 I was selected to be in the inaugural cohort at NEW INC at the New Museum, in NYC. For the year, I used NEW INC as my studio, and my desk was located next to the Rhizome editorial team. This was fortuitous; after learning about my work and ongoing conversations about digital art, I began writing for Rhizome, which eventually led to my serving as a Contributing Editor and curator of The Download. I wrote a total of 13 pieces and commissioned six new works of net art during a two-year period.
