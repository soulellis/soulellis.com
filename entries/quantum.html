Notes on Quantum Queerness (2024)

Deck of 30 cards
Wrapped in a one-page folding zine
Risograph printing
Rubber band binding
4 in. x 6 in. folded
Edition of 40

Notes on Quantum Queerness was prepared for Toolkit for Today: Cross Wor(l)ds/Queer Wor(l)ds at the Canadian Centre for Architecture in Montr√©al, Canada, held in 22 July 2024. The event explored imaginative, material, and methodological paths to collectively navigate how queer and trans* theory can offer critiques of power that allow for the revision of some of the core concepts and ontologies of architectural history. It was organized by Malcolm Rio and S.E. Eisterer, and I was invited to speak about my practice and current research. The publication—in the form of an unfolding deck of cards, wrapped in a one-page zine—was distributed to all participants.
