Screen Zine (2020)

120-page book
Risograph printed
Perfect bound
8.5 in. x 11 in.
Edition of 100

Screen Zine is the result of a one-hour workshop conducted online for the Brooklyn Art Book Fair on Saturday, June 27, 2020 at 2:00PM. The workshop opened with 1-minute attunement exercises, as an experiment to gather and presence everyone into a digital screen-based space. The 20 participants then composed images and messages on their desktops for about 20 minutes. Language from Urgency Reader 2 was provided for possible inspiration. Participants shared their screens and the desktops were captured as images. Later, those images were mixed and composed into 3-color risograph prints, and assembled into an edition of 30 zines. Each participant received a copy of Screen Zine in the mail.
