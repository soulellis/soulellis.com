Tiny Digest #1 (SPACE) (2021)

1-page folded zine
Risograph printing
11 in. x 17 in.
Edition of 100

Tiny Digest #1 was a slow, distributed-by-hand Queer.Archive.Work newsletter printed on a single sheet of cheap paper, designed to be assembled quickly, easily printed, and mailed out in an ordinary business envelope. I created an open call to the QAW community, resulting in 23 contributions around the theme of space—queer space, taking up space, sacred space, porous space, safe space, outer space, spaced out, shared space, screen space, etc.
